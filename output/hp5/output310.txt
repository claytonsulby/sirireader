 “What’s that? Who must have sent them?” “Lord Voldemort,” said Harry. He registered dimly how strange it was that the Dursleys, who flinched, winced, and squawked if they heard words like “wizard,” “magic,” or “wand,” could hear the name of the most evil wizard of all time without the slightest tremor. “Lord — hang on,” said Uncle Vernon, his face screwed up, a look of dawning comprehension in his piggy eyes.
