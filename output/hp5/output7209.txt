 The recent blow on the back of Harry’s head seemed to have unsteadied him; he narrowed his eyes, swaying slightly, until the walls stopped moving again. With a sinking heart Harry saw that Hermione’s fiery crosses had faded from the doors. “So which way d’you reck — ?” But before they could make a decision as to which way to try, a door to their right sprang open and three people fell out of it.
