 Together with Ron they had spent most of the day seeking out those people who had signed their names to the list in the Hog’s Head and telling them where to meet that evening. Somewhat to Harry’s disappointment, it was Ginny who managed to find Cho Chang and her friend first; however, by the end of dinner he was confident that the news had been passed to every one of the twenty-five people who had turned up in the Hog’s Head. At half-past seven Harry, Ron, and Hermione left the Gryffindor  DUMBLEDORE’S ARMY common room, Harry clutching a certain piece of aged parchment in his hand.
