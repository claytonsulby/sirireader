 As he put his books away, Ron, who was dozing lightly in an armchair, gave a muffled grunt, awoke, looked blearily into the fire and said, “Sirius!” Harry whipped around; Sirius’s untidy dark head was sitting in the fire again. “Hi,” he said, grinning. “Hi,” chorused Harry, Ron, and Hermione, all three kneeling down upon the hearthrug.
