 He knew he was dead: it was pain beyond imagining, pain past endurance — He was gone from the hall, he was locked in the coils of a creature   with red eyes, so tightly bound that Harry did not know where his body ended and the creature’s began. They were fused together, bound by pain, and there was no escape — And when the creature spoke, it used Harry’s mouth, so that in his agony he felt his jaw move. .
