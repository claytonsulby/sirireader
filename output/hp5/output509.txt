  NUMBER TWELVE, GRIMMAULD PLACE Hermione, still beaming, let go of Harry, but before she could say another word there was a soft whooshing sound and something white soared from the top of a dark wardrobe and landed gently on Harry’s shoulder. “Hedwig!” The snowy owl clicked her beak and nibbled his ear affectionately as Harry stroked her feathers. “She’s been in a right state,” said Ron.
