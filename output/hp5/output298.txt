 Wasn’t anybody going to say “well done” for fighting off two dementors single-handedly? Both Mr. Weasley and Sirius were acting as though he’d misbehaved and they were saving their tellings-off until they could ascertain how much damage had been done. “— a peck, I mean, pack of owls shooting in and out of my house and I won’t have it, boy, I won’t —” “I can’t stop the owls coming,” Harry snapped, crushing Sirius’s letter in his fist.
