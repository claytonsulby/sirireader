 Behind them, Umbridge tripped over a fallen sapling. Neither of them paused to help her up again; Hermione merely strode on, calling loudly over her shoulder, “It’s a bit further in!” “Hermione, keep your voice down,” Harry muttered, hurrying to catch up with her. “Anything could be listening in here —” “I want us heard,” she answered quietly, as Umbridge jogged noisily after them.
