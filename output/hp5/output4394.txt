 Harry could hear his voice echoing up through the floor in the cold and empty drawing room where he was sitting alone, watching the sky outside the windows growing whiter, threatening snow, all the time feeling a savage pleasure that he was giving the others the opportunity to keep talking sure that he was giving the others the opportunity to keep talking about him, as they were bound to be doing. When he heard Mrs.   Weasley calling his name softly up the stairs around lunchtime he retreated farther upstairs and ignored her.
