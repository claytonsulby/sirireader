 Two beds along was a woman whose entire head was covered in fur; Harry remembered something     similar happening to Hermione during their second year, although fortunately the damage, in her case, had not been permanent. At the far end of the ward flowery curtains had been drawn around two beds to give the occupants and their visitors some privacy. “Here you are, Agnes,” said the Healer brightly to the furry-faced woman, handing her a small pile of Christmas presents.
