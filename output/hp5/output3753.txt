 “An’ you too, Neville, eh? An’ —” “Excuse me,” said Malfoy in a sneering voice, “but what exactly are we supposed to be seeing?” For answer, Hagrid pointed at the cow carcass on the ground. The whole class stared at it for a few seconds, then several people gasped and Parvati squealed. Harry understood why: Bits of flesh stripping themselves away from the bones and vanishing into thin air had to look very odd indeed.
