The hat considered for a moment, then the rip near the brim opened again and shouted, “GRYFFINDOR !” Harry clapped loudly with the rest of Gryffindor House as Euan Abercrombie staggered to their table and sat down, looking as though he would like very much to sink through the floor and never be looked at again.
