 “Good to see you!” Over his shoulder Harry saw Bill, who still wore his long hair in a ponytail, hastily rolling up the lengths of parchment left on the table. “Journey all right, Harry?” Bill called, trying to gather up twelve scrolls at once. “Mad-Eye didn’t make you come via Greenland, then?” “He tried,” said Tonks, striding over to help Bill and immediately sending a candle toppling onto the last piece of parchment.
