 “In fact, the more impressive the worse it is, I would have thought, given that the boy did it in plain view of a Muggle!” Those who had been frowning now murmured in agreement, but it was the sight of Percy’s sanctimonious little nod that goaded Harry into speech. “I did it because of the dementors!” he said loudly, before anyone could interrupt him again.
