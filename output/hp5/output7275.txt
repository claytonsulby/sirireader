 As Harry scrambled up again he looked around and saw Malfoy smash into the dais on which Sirius and Bellatrix were now dueling. Malfoy aimed his wand at Harry and Neville again, but before he could draw breath to strike, Lupin had jumped between them. “Harry, round up the others and GO!” Harry seized Neville by the shoulder of his robes and lifted him bodily onto the first tier of stone steps.
